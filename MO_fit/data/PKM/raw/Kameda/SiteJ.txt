DESCRIPTION: 
Melt percentage data from Site J Ice Core, South Greenland, and June 
temperature reconstruction for Jakobshavn, west coast of Greenland.
Two cores were drilled 1m. apart, to 101.8 m. and 206.6 m., by the 
Japanese Arctic Glaciological Expedition (JAGE89).  2804 melt features 
with a total thickness of 30.32 m. were observed in the 206.6  m. core.

Site J Ice Core:  66deg 51.9'N, 46deg 15.9'W, 2030m.
Jakobshavn:  69deg 13'N, 51deg 6'W, 30m.
